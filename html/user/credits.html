Dennis Payne is a graduate from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. He currently works for Skillsoft. Game development has always interested Dennis. While at RPI he worked with two friends to develop Legend of Zelda for the PC. Unfortunately the project fell apart but he learned valuable experience. He started Troll Bridge to provide a more object oriented framework with customization through dynamically linked libraries. He can be emailed at dulsi@identicalsoftware.com

madameberry provided the music for Troll Bridge. Boot Up and Omen are used on the surface and the dungeons respectively.
